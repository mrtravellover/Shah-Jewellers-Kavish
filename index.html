Our Story

Three generations. One promise.

For over 50 years, the Shah family has been part of everyday life in Jhabua. It began with my grandfather and a humble flour mill, grew into a trusted provision store under my father — Shah Kirana Stores — and today continues into its third generation.

On 22 February 2024, I carried our family name into a new craft. Shah Jewellers opened its doors, bringing five decades of honest dealing to fine silver, gemstones and pure metals.

What we sell has changed across generations; what we stand for has not. Every coin, every ornament and every gemstone carries the same promise our family started with — honest weight, certified quality and a fair price. That is our Tradition of Trust.
